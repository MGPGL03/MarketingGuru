Facebook Ads
Recommendations: Lead Generation Efficiency: In September, leads decreased by 36.0% and cost per acquisition (CPA) rose to ฿1115.96 from ฿702.48 in August, indicating a significant decline in lead generation efficiency despite a 1.7% increase in spend. Focus on optimizing campaigns specifically for lead quality and conversion; review targeting and ad creatives for the campaigns driving leads to identify and rectify underperforming elements. Messaging Engagement Performance: Messaging conversations increased by 14.5% in September, while the cost per messaging conversation decreased by 11.2%, moving from ฿68.60 in August to ฿60.94. Capitalize on this positive trend by allocating more budget towards high-performing messaging campaigns such as "Inbox_new coat B2b" and "Inbox_Denzi pic", which show significantly lower costs per conversation, and re-evaluate or pause the "awarness_new coat B2c" ad due to its exceptionally high cost per messaging conversation of ฿1722.95. Overall: September saw a 1.7% increase in spend on Facebook Ads, however, impressions dropped by 13.4% and clicks by 0.1%. While the click-through rate (CTR) improved by 15.3% to 0.7%, lead generation declined significantly by 36.0%, increasing the cost per acquisition (CPA). Conversely, messaging conversations grew by 14.5%, and their cost efficiency improved by 11.2%.
Key Takeaways
The primary challenge in September was the substantial decrease in lead volume and the corresponding increase in CPA, which demands immediate attention. Despite this, the growth in messaging conversations and the improved cost efficiency in this area present a strong opportunity for engagement and potential conversions. The disparity in creative performance highlights the need for a granular review of campaign-level effectiveness to reallocate resources strategically and improve overall ROI.
