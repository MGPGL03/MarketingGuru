Executive Insights

Google Ads •Spend: ฿36,069.67 (-38.1%) •Impressions decreased (-38.0%), leading to fewer Clicks (-13.9%) and Conversions (-25.4%). •Efficiency improved, with CTR rising (+38.8%) and Cost per conversion decreasing from ฿434.71 to ฿360.70. ✅ Overall: Lower spend significantly reduced overall volume, but the platform demonstrated improved efficiency per interaction and conversion.
Recommendation: Scale Efficient Campaigns
Given the improved CTR and reduced CPA, the current campaigns are efficient. Increase spend on the top-performing campaigns to recover lost impressions, clicks, and conversions, maximizing the proven efficiency to drive higher overall volume. Facebook Ads •Spend: ฿1,362.7 (-50.8%) •Impressions grew strongly (+43.0%), and Clicks increased significantly (+309.7%), with CTR up by 186.5%. •Leads dropped drastically to zero (-100.0%), and Messaging Conversations decreased (-91.4%). •Cost per messaging conversation surged from ฿79.19 to ฿454.23 (+473.6%). ✅ Overall: While engagement metrics soared at a reduced spend, the platform failed to generate leads, and messaging conversations became significantly more expensive.
Recommendation: Re-evaluate Conversion Strategy
Investigate the severe drop in leads and messaging conversations immediately. Review ad creatives, targeting parameters, and call-to-action for lead generation campaigns. Consider A/B testing new lead forms or conversion pathways to identify and resolve conversion blockers. Prioritize optimizing for lead quality and reducing the cost per messaging conversation. Recommendation: Optimize Organic Search Performance
Despite the significant increase in overall keyword traffic, organic sessions slightly declined. Focus on optimizing top landing pages (e.g., "/help/kb/systems/os/how-to-reset-windows-10-using-command-prompt" and homepage) to improve their performance in search results and convert the increased keyword visibility into higher organic sessions and new user acquisition. 🔎 Key Takeaways Google Ads demonstrated strong efficiency, suggesting an opportunity to scale. For SEO, focus on converting improved keyword visibility into higher organic sessions and new users.
