Executive Insights

Google Ads
•Spend: THB 18,034.83 (-39.9%) •Impressions declined by 51.3%, showing reduced reach. •Clicks decreased by 24.8%, but CTR improved significantly (+54.5%) to 7.8%. •Conversions decreased by 35.9%, however, efficiency improved: Cost per conversion (CPA) decreased by 6.3% to ฿360.70. ✅ Overall: Google Ads delivered conversions more efficiently at a lower cost, despite a reduction in overall volume due to decreased spend. Recommendation: Optimize for Volume & Efficiency To leverage the improved efficiency, consider gradually increasing the budget for Google Ads, focusing on campaigns that demonstrate the lowest CPA to regain lost impressions and clicks while maintaining a strong conversion rate.
Facebook Ads
•Spend: THB 1,362.70 (-50.8%) •Impressions grew strongly (+43.0%), showing broader reach. •Clicks increased dramatically (+309.7%), and CTR improved significantly (+186.5%) to 8.1%. •Leads decreased (-100.0%) to 0, and messaging conversations also dropped sharply (-91.4%). •Costs: Cost per messaging conversation rose drastically (+473.6%) to ฿454.23. ✅ Overall: Engagement improved significantly, but lead generation and conversation quality severely declined, indicating a critical issue in the conversion funnel. Recommendation: Re-evaluate Conversion Strategy A thorough review of Facebook's conversion objectives, targeting, ad creatives, and lead forms is critical. Focus on identifying and fixing conversion funnel issues, potentially piloting new campaign types or ad formats aimed specifically at lead capture.
