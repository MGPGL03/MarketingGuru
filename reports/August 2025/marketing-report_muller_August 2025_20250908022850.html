Executive Insights

Google Ads •Spend: THB 7,434.19 (-6.8%) •Impressions declined significantly (-41.5%), indicating reduced reach. •Clicks also saw a decrease (-6.7%), however, Conversions increased by 31.1%. •Efficiency improved as CTR rose by 59.5%, and Cost per Conversion decreased from ฿88.64 to ฿63.00 (-28.9%). ✅ Overall: Google Ads delivered more conversions at a significantly lower cost per conversion, despite a reduction in overall reach and clicks. Recommendation: Focus on optimizing campaigns to regain impression share and clicks without sacrificing the improved conversion efficiency. Analyze which specific ad groups or keywords contributed to the higher conversions at a lower CPA and consider allocating more budget to them. Facebook Ads •Spend: THB 2,354.52 (-3.5%) •Impressions grew strongly (+60.5%), showing broader reach. •Clicks increased by 8.9%, but CTR declined (-32.2%). •Leads decreased by 20.0%, and Cost per Lead increased to ฿588.63 (+20.6%). •Messaging conversations slightly increased (+5.1%), and Cost per Messaging Conversation improved (-8.2%). ✅ Overall: While Facebook Ads achieved greater reach and slightly more messaging conversations at a lower cost, lead generation declined with increased cost per lead. Recommendation: Review creative performance and targeting for lead generation campaigns, as impressions and clicks increased but leads decreased. The ad "อยากติดลิฟท์บ้าน" performed best in messaging conversations, consider scaling similar successful creatives. Evaluate the landing page experience for lead forms to improve conversion rates. SEO •Users: 1,037 (-14.2%) •New Users: 995 (-11.3%) •Sessions: 1,058 (-15.5%) •Organic Sessions: 312 (-30.7%) •Engagement Rate: 17.9% (-15.9%) ✅ Overall: There was a general decline in overall user engagement and organic sessions. However, several key keywords saw significant improvements in ranking and traffic. "ลิฟท์" moved from position 17 to 9, increasing traffic by 94, and "ลิฟต์โดยสาร" improved from position 20 to 11, adding 12 traffic. Total SEO traffic increased by 179.7%. Recommendation: Despite an overall drop in GA4 metrics, the strong keyword performance is a positive signal. Prioritize content creation and on-page optimization around the keywords that are gaining traction ("ลิฟท์," "ลิฟต์โดยสาร," "ลิฟท์ขนของขนาดเล็ก") to capitalize on improved rankings and drive more organic traffic. Investigate the reasons for the overall decline in users, sessions, and engagement rate by analyzing other traffic sources and site-wide issues. 🔎 Key Takeaways Google Ads demonstrated improved efficiency, yielding more conversions at a lower cost despite reduced reach. Facebook Ads achieved greater reach but struggled with lead quality and conversion. SEO saw a decline in overall site metrics but strong improvements in key keyword rankings and organic traffic. Next month, we should leverage Google Ads' conversion efficiency by strategically scaling successful campaigns. For Facebook Ads, a thorough review of lead generation campaign creatives, targeting, and landing pages is crucial to improve lead quality and conversion rates. Additionally, focus on optimizing content for the high-performing SEO keywords to convert improved rankings into sustained organic growth.
