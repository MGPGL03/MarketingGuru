Executive Insights

Google Ads •Spend: THB 9,834.38 (-17.0%). •Impressions decreased (-36.8%), indicating reduced reach. •Clicks also declined (-29.3%), but the CTR increased (+11.9%). •Conversions significantly improved (+79.1%), while Cost Per Acquisition decreased to ฿289.25 from ฿624.42. ✅ Overall: Although reach and clicks decreased, Google Ads delivered significantly more conversions at a much lower cost per acquisition. We should Analyze Google Ads campaign targeting and budget allocation to maintain conversion efficiency while aiming to recover lost impressions and clicks. This could involve identifying underperforming keywords or ad groups and reallocating budget to top-performing areas, especially the "Per_MAX_คู่แข่ง" campaign which shows strong conversion performance. Facebook Ads •Spend: THB 14,076.80 (-2.0%). •Impressions increased (+14.3%), and Clicks also rose (+6.6%). •Leads dramatically increased (+550.0%) from 4 to 26, leading to a significant drop in Cost Per Lead from ฿3590.64 to ฿541.42. •Messaging conversations also grew (+53.3%), with Cost Per Messaging Conversation decreasing (-36.0%) from ฿156.11 to ฿99.84. ✅ Overall: Facebook Ads showed strong growth in both leads and messaging conversations, with improved efficiency in both cost metrics. We should Capitalize on the positive momentum in Facebook Ads by scaling successful ad sets and creatives, particularly those driving leads and messaging conversations. Review the top-performing creatives (carousel and video ads) and campaigns to replicate their success and optimize for further growth. Key Takeaways Google Ads is driving highly efficient conversions despite a reduction in overall reach and clicks. Facebook Ads demonstrated significant improvement in lead generation and engagement, with better cost efficiency. Next month, we should focus on Optimizing Google Ads for broader reach without sacrificing conversion efficiency, potentially by testing new ad formats or expanding keyword targeting selectively. Simultaneously, we should Expand Facebook Ads campaigns that are currently delivering strong lead and messaging conversation performance, ensuring sustained growth and cost efficiency. We should also Investigate the synergy between platforms to understand how increased reach on Facebook could potentially support the highly efficient conversion funnel on Google, and vice versa.
