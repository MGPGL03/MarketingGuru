Executive Insights

Google Ads
•Spend: THB 7,434.19 (-6.8%) •Impressions declined significantly (-41.5%), indicating reduced reach. •Clicks decreased by 6.7%, but Conversions improved (+31.1%). •Efficiency improved: Cost per conversion decreased from ฿88.64 to ฿63.00, a 28.9% improvement. ✅ Overall: While reach and clicks are down, Google Ads is converting more efficiently at a lower cost per conversion. Recommendation: Optimize for Reach and Scale Conversion Campaigns Focus on strategies to increase impressions and clicks without compromising the improved conversion efficiency. Consider expanding keywords, adjusting bid strategies for broader reach, and testing new ad creatives to capture attention. Simultaneously, scale the best-performing conversion campaigns to leverage the lower cost per conversion.
Facebook Ads
•Spend: THB 2,354.52 (-3.5%). •Impressions grew strongly (+60.5%), showing broader reach. •Clicks increased by 8.9%, but Leads decreased (-20.0%). •Messaging conversations slightly increased (+5.1%). •Costs: Cost per lead rose (+20.6%) from ฿488.18 to ฿588.63, but Cost per messaging conversation improved significantly (-8.2%) from ฿62.59 to ฿57.43. ✅ Overall: Facebook Ads achieved higher reach and engagement but struggled with lead generation quality, while messaging conversations show better efficiency. Recommendation: Enhance Lead Quality and Leverage Messaging Investigate the decline in lead volume and the increase in cost per lead. Review lead form questions, targeting parameters, and creative messaging to ensure higher quality leads. Given the improved cost per messaging conversation, explore further optimization and scaling of messaging campaigns as a viable conversion path.
Key Takeaways
Google Ads is driving conversions more efficiently, despite a drop in overall reach and clicks. Facebook Ads is excelling in reach and engagement, but lead generation is a concern, while messaging is a positive. Next Steps: Reallocate and Refine Consider reallocating a portion of the Facebook Ads budget, especially from underperforming lead generation efforts, towards scaling successful Google Ads campaigns to maximize conversions. For Facebook, focus on refining lead generation strategies to improve quality and explore increasing investment in the more efficient messaging campaigns.
