Executive Insights

Google Ads
In August, Google Ads saw a significant reduction in spend by 38.1% compared to July, leading to a decrease in impressions (-38.0%), clicks (-13.9%), and conversions (-25.4%). However, the Click-Through Rate (CTR) improved by 38.8%, and the Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) decreased from ฿434.71 to ฿360.70, indicating better efficiency per conversion. To capitalize on the improved efficiency, it is recommended to strategically increase the budget in August, focusing on top-performing campaigns and keywords that delivered a lower CPA, to regain impression and click volume without sacrificing efficiency.
Facebook Ads
Facebook Ads experienced a 50.8% decrease in spend in August. Despite this, impressions surged by 43.0% and clicks by an impressive 309.7%, resulting in a 186.5% increase in CTR. However, this high engagement did not translate into conversions, with leads dropping by 100.0% (from 6 to 0) and messaging conversations decreasing by 91.4%, alongside a substantial 473.6% increase in cost per messaging conversation. It is crucial to immediately investigate the drastic decline in leads and messaging conversations. Review campaign objectives, audience targeting, ad creatives, and lead form functionality to ensure high engagement converts into valuable business outcomes, reallocating the budget from underperforming conversion campaigns.
SEO
For August, SEO performance showed a slight increase in users (+0.3%), sessions (+0.6%), and event count (+5.3%), coupled with a strong 14.9% improvement in engagement rate. While organic sessions slightly declined by 3.5%, overall keyword traffic increased by 10.6%. Keyword analysis reveals that four keywords improved in rank, but eight declined. To build on the positive engagement and traffic trends, it is recommended to focus on optimizing content and backlink strategies for the keywords that declined in rank, especially those with high search volume. Additionally, leverage the top-performing landing pages to further enhance user experience and potential conversions. 🔎 Key Takeaways Google Ads demonstrated improved efficiency per conversion in August despite reduced spend and volume; the focus for next month should be on scaling this efficiency. Facebook Ads generated significant engagement but completely failed to convert into leads or effective messaging conversations, requiring an urgent review of its conversion funnel. SEO maintained stability with positive engagement and overall traffic growth, but requires attention to declining keyword rankings to sustain organic visibility. To maximize overall marketing impact, we should allocate additional budget to Google Ads where efficiency has improved, and conduct an immediate, thorough audit of Facebook Ads to diagnose and fix the conversion issues.
