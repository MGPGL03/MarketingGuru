Executive Insights

Google Ads •Spend: THB 7,434.19 (-6.8%) •Impressions declined significantly (-41.5%), indicating reduced reach. •Clicks decreased (-6.7%), but Conversions improved (+31.1%). •Efficiency improved: Cost per conversion dropped from ฿88.64 to ฿63.00 (-28.9%), and CTR increased by 59.5%. ✅ Overall: Google Ads had reduced reach and clicks but significantly improved conversion efficiency and volume at a lower cost per conversion. Optimize Google Ads for Scaled Conversion Volume. While reach and clicks declined, the strong improvement in conversion rate and reduction in CPA suggest high-quality traffic and effective ad copy. Focus on identifying the factors behind the impression and click decline (e.g., budget caps, audience saturation, keyword performance) and scale successful campaigns, particularly "Muller-14May," to capitalize on the improved conversion efficiency. Facebook Ads •Spend: THB 2,354.52 (-3.5%) •Impressions grew strongly (+60.5%), showing broader reach. •Clicks increased by 8.9%, but CTR declined (-32.2%). •Leads decreased (-20.0%), and Cost per Lead increased (+20.6%). •Messaging conversations increased (+5.1%), and Cost per messaging conversation improved significantly (-8.2%). ✅ Overall: Facebook Ads delivered higher reach and engagement (clicks, messaging conversations) but struggled with lead generation efficiency. Enhance Facebook Lead Quality and Conversion Rate. Despite increased impressions and clicks, the drop in CTR and decline in leads, coupled with increased CPA for leads, indicate a potential misalignment between ad content/targeting and lead quality. Review ad creatives, especially those with high spend but low engagement/conversions, and refine audience targeting for lead generation campaigns. Prioritize the best performing creative "อยากติดลิฟท์บ้าน" and campaigns like "Inbox muller" for messaging conversions, which show better efficiency. SEO •Users: No data •New Users: No data •Sessions: No data ✅ Overall: No data available for analysis. Implement SEO Tracking and Strategy. Without any data, it's impossible to assess SEO performance. Implement robust SEO tracking tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Google Search Console) to gather data on organic traffic, user behavior, and keyword rankings to inform future strategy and identify growth opportunities. 🔎 Key Takeaways Google Ads is demonstrating strong conversion efficiency and improved CPA despite reduced top-funnel metrics. Facebook Ads is excelling in reach and initial engagement but faces challenges in lead generation quality and cost-effectiveness. Messaging conversations show promise. SEO performance remains unmeasured, indicating a critical gap in understanding organic channel contribution. Next month, we should prioritize scaling successful Google Ads conversion campaigns while conducting a deep dive into Facebook Ads lead generation to optimize targeting and creative strategy. Simultaneously, establishing SEO tracking is crucial to build a holistic marketing view.
