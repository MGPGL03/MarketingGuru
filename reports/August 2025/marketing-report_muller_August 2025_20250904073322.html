Executive Insights

Google Ads
• Spend: THB 7,434.19 (-6.8%). • Impressions decreased by 41.5% and Clicks by 6.7%, indicating reduced reach. • Conversions increased significantly by 31.1% with CTR improving by 59.5%. • Cost per conversion (CPA) decreased from ฿88.64 to ฿63.00, an improvement of 28.9%. ✅ Overall: Despite lower reach, Google Ads drove more conversions at a lower cost, demonstrating increased efficiency in August compared to July.
Facebook Ads
• Spend: THB 2,354.52 (-3.5%). • Impressions increased by 60.5% and Clicks by 8.9%, showing expanded reach. • Leads decreased by 20.0%, and CTR declined by 32.2%. • Messaging conversations increased by 5.1%, with cost per messaging conversation improving by 8.2%. ✅ Overall: Facebook Ads successfully expanded reach and engagement at a lower spend, but lead generation declined, suggesting a need to refine conversion paths in August compared to July.
SEO
• Users: 1,037 (-14.2%) • New Users: 995 (-11.3%) • Sessions: 1,058 (-15.5%) • Organic Sessions: 312 (-30.7%) • Engagement Rate: 17.9% (-15.9%) • Overall keyword traffic improved significantly by 179.7%, with key terms like "ลิฟท์" showing strong position and traffic gains, moving from rank 17 to 9 and traffic increasing from 20 to 114. ✅ Overall: August saw a general decline in GA4 website traffic metrics; however, impressive gains in keyword rankings and organic traffic for critical terms highlight a positive underlying SEO trajectory compared to July.
Key Takeaways
Google Ads continues to demonstrate strong performance, delivering more conversions at a reduced cost per acquisition. Facebook Ads has effectively broadened its reach and improved engagement for messaging conversations but is experiencing challenges in generating qualified leads. Meanwhile, SEO, despite an overall decrease in website traffic, is achieving significant progress in key keyword rankings and organic traffic.
1. Optimize Google Ads for Conversion Volume
Given the substantial increase in conversions and improved cost-per-conversion, allocate additional budget and expand successful Google Ads campaigns to capture more converting traffic, leveraging the efficiency gains observed.
2. Enhance Facebook Lead Generation Strategy
Investigate the decline in leads on Facebook Ads by reviewing targeting parameters, ad creatives, and lead form effectiveness. Implement A/B testing on different approaches to improve lead quality and conversion rates.
3. Capitalize on SEO Keyword Momentum
Amplify content creation and on-page optimization efforts for keywords that have shown significant ranking improvements, such as "ลิฟท์". Develop dedicated landing pages or blog posts to maximize organic traffic conversion for these high-performing terms.
