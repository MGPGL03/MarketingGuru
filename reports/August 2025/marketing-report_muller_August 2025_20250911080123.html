Executive Insights

Google Ads
Spend decreased to THB 36,069.67 (-38.1%), resulting in fewer impressions (-38.0%) and clicks (-13.9%). Conversions also saw a decline (-25.4%), reaching 100. However, the Click-Through Rate (CTR) improved significantly to 7.8% (+38.8%), and the Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) became more efficient at THB 360.70 (-16.9%). This indicates that while volume decreased, the quality of engagement and cost efficiency per conversion improved. Recommendation 1: Review and scale top-performing campaigns. Despite overall declines in volume metrics, the improved CTR and CPA suggest underlying efficiency. Identify the campaigns or ad groups that maintained better performance or contributed to the improved efficiency (e.g., lower CPA). Allocate more budget to these successful areas to regain impression and click volume without sacrificing cost-effectiveness. Investigate if budget reductions were the primary cause of the decline in volume and conversions, and consider increasing spend in profitable campaigns.
Facebook Ads
Impressions grew by 43.0% to 34,605, and clicks surged by 309.7% to 2,794, leading to a substantial increase in CTR to 8.1% (+186.5%). However, leads dropped dramatically by 100.0% (from 6 to 0), and messaging conversations decreased by 91.4% (from 35 to 3). The cost per messaging conversation increased significantly by 473.6% to THB 454.23. Despite improved initial engagement (impressions, clicks, CTR), there's a critical issue in converting this interest into valuable actions at an acceptable cost. Recommendation 2: Re-evaluate lead generation and messaging strategies. The large increase in impressions and clicks, coupled with a drastic drop in leads and messaging conversations, indicates a disconnect between engagement and conversion. Analyze creative performance and targeting for lead generation campaigns. Focus on optimizing the ad copy, visuals, and calls-to-action to better align with conversion goals. For messaging campaigns, review the automated responses and overall user experience to improve conversation rates and reduce the high cost per messaging conversation. Consider A/B testing different approaches to identify what resonates best with the target audience for actual conversions.
SEO
Users slightly increased by 0.3% to 6,274, and sessions also saw a minor rise of 0.6% to 6,542. The engagement rate improved by 14.9% to 31.0%, and the total event count increased by 5.3% to 27,354. However, new users decreased by 2.5%, and organic sessions declined by 3.5% to 4,183. Keyword performance shows mixed results with 4 keywords improving position, 8 declining, and 2 remaining unchanged, but overall traffic from keywords increased by 10.6%. Recommendation 3: Optimize for organic growth and keyword relevance. While overall traffic from keywords increased, the decline in organic sessions and new users suggests a need for strategic SEO adjustments. Focus on improving rankings for the 8 declining keywords by refreshing content, building high-quality backlinks, and ensuring technical SEO best practices. Analyze the search intent behind keywords driving traffic to high-performing landing pages (e.g., "/help/kb/systems/os/how-to-reset-windows-10-using-command-prompt") and replicate successful content strategies across other relevant pages. Prioritize content creation around high-volume, relevant keywords with good traffic potential to attract more new users and organic sessions.
Key Takeaways
Google Ads experienced a decline in volume but maintained efficiency with better CTR and CPA. Facebook Ads saw strong top-of-funnel engagement, but conversions (leads and messaging conversations) significantly dropped, indicating a need for conversion optimization. SEO showed a slight overall traffic increase from keywords, but a dip in new organic users and sessions, highlighting areas for content and ranking improvement. For the upcoming month, it is crucial to analyze Google Ads' performance dips to recover volume, while rigorously testing and optimizing Facebook Ads' lead and messaging campaigns to improve conversion quality and efficiency. Simultaneously, enhance SEO efforts on declining keywords and prioritize content that drives new organic users.
