Executive Insights

Google Ads
•Spend: THB 7,434.19 (-6.8%) •Impressions declined by 41.5%, indicating reduced reach. •Clicks also decreased by 6.7%, but Conversions significantly improved (+31.1%). •Efficiency improved: Click-through rate (CTR) increased by 59.5%, and Cost per conversion (CPA) decreased by 28.92% (from ฿88.64 to ฿63.00). ✅ Overall: Google Ads delivered more conversions at a lower cost, despite a decrease in impressions and clicks, suggesting highly targeted and efficient spending.
Facebook Ads
•Spend: THB 2,354.52 (-3.5%) •Impressions grew strongly (+60.5%), showing broader reach. •Clicks increased by 8.9%, but Leads decreased (-20.0%). •Messaging conversations also increased (+5.1%). •Costs: Cost per lead rose (+20.57%, from ฿488.18 to ฿588.63), but Cost per messaging conversation improved (-8.2%, from ฿62.59 to ฿57.43). ✅ Overall: Facebook Ads increased reach and engagement (clicks, messaging conversations) but faced challenges in converting to qualified leads, with an increased cost per lead.
SEO
•Users: 1,037 (-14.2%) •New Users: 995 (-11.3%) •Sessions: 1,058 (-15.5%) •Organic Sessions: 312 (-30.7%) •Engagement Rate: 17.9% (-15.9%) •Event Count: 3,709 (-20.6%) ✅ Overall: SEO performance declined across all key metrics for users and sessions, especially organic sessions. While some keywords improved in ranking, this did not translate to overall organic traffic growth. 🔎 Key Takeaways Google Ads is performing efficiently, driving more conversions at lower costs. Facebook Ads is delivering engagement but struggling to convert into qualified leads. Overall SEO organic traffic is declining, necessitating an investigation. Next month, we should scale Google Ads campaigns to maximize strong performance, optimize Facebook lead campaigns (review targeting, creatives, and lead forms), and conduct a comprehensive SEO audit to reverse the decline in organic sessions and capitalize on improving keyword rankings.
