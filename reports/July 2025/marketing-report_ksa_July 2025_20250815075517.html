Executive Insights

Facebook Recommendations

Review Lead Generation Strategy: The considerable decline in leads by 33.3% from June to July, alongside an increase in the cost per lead from ‡∏ø2724.59 to ‡∏ø3590.64, highlights a critical area for improvement. Focus on analyzing underperforming campaigns and creatives within Facebook Ads. Implement A/B testing for new ad copy, visuals, and refined audience targeting to enhance lead quality and volume, aiming to reduce the cost per lead to levels observed in June or even lower.

Google Recommendations

Optimize Ad Relevance and Quality: Although Google Ads saw positive increases in conversions (18.5%), impressions (135.0%), and clicks (53.2%) from June to July, the Click-Through Rate (CTR) experienced a significant drop from 4.8% to 3.1% (a 34.8% decrease). This suggests that while reach expanded, the ads might be less relevant or engaging to the broader audience. It is recommended to analyze existing ad copy, headlines, and descriptions, and test new variations to improve ad relevance and engagement, thereby boosting CTR. Re-evaluating keyword targeting can also ensure that the increased traffic is of higher quality.

General Recommendations

Strategic Budget Reallocation: Considering Google Ads' improved Cost Per Acquisition (CPA decreased from ‡∏ø692.87 in June to ‡∏ø624.42 in July) and consistent conversion growth, juxtaposed with Facebook Ads' higher CPA and declining lead volume, a strategic reallocation of budget warrants consideration. Shifting a portion of the current Facebook budget towards Google Ads could leverage the more cost-effective conversion path on Google, optimizing the overall campaign efficiency and maximizing lead generation across platforms.
