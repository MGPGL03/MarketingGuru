Executive Insights

Facebook Recommendations

Optimize Facebook Campaign Structure. In May, Facebook spend increased by 20% compared to April, while revenue only saw an 8% increase. This led to a decrease in Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) from 2.5 to 2.25. It is recommended to conduct a thorough audit of Facebook campaign structures, focusing on audience targeting, creative fatigue, and bid strategies. Identifying underperforming ad sets and reallocating budget to more efficient campaigns can help improve ROAS and ensure a better return on increased investment.

Google Recommendations

Scale High-Performing Google Campaigns. Google's performance in May significantly improved, with a 20% decrease in spend alongside a 12% increase in revenue, driving ROAS from 2.5 to an impressive 3.5. This indicates strong efficiency and a high potential for scalability. It is recommended to strategically increase budget allocation to the top-performing Google campaigns and keywords that contributed to this efficiency gain. Further investment in these areas is likely to yield substantial returns and capitalize on current momentum.

General Recommendations

Strategic Budget Reallocation. Given the contrasting performance between Facebook and Google, a strategic reallocation of advertising budget should be considered. While Facebook requires optimization to improve efficiency, Google demonstrates robust performance even with reduced spend. Redirecting a portion of the budget from underperforming Facebook segments towards Google's high-efficiency campaigns could maximize overall advertising effectiveness and drive greater total revenue for the upcoming period. This approach ensures that resources are invested where they yield the best return.
