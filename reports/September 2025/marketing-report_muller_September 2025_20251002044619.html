Executive Insights

Google Ads
•Spend: THB 8,019.22 (+7.9%) •Impressions: 9,386 (-19.0%) •Clicks: 858 (-34.0%) •Conversions: 50 (-57.6%) •CPA: ฿160.38 (+154.6%) ✅ Overall: Google Ads experienced a significant decline in efficiency and conversions despite increased spend in September compared to August. Recommendation 1: Address Conversion and Click Decline Google Ads saw a dramatic drop in conversions (-57.6%) and clicks (-34.0%) in September, while spend increased by 7.9%, resulting in a 154.6% rise in Cost Per Acquisition (CPA). This indicates a severe decline in campaign effectiveness. Immediately review the "Muller-14May" campaign's recent performance. Investigate changes in ad copy, keyword targeting, bid strategies, and landing page relevance to understand the root cause of the conversion drop. Recommendation 2: Optimize High-Cost Search Terms The top search terms like "aritco lift ราคา" and "ลิ ฟ ท์ ขน สินค้า" are driving the majority of clicks and conversions but the overall CPA increase signals inefficiency. Analyze the Cost Per Conversion (CPA) for individual keywords to identify any that are disproportionately expensive and reallocate budget or adjust bids accordingly to improve efficiency. Recommendation 3: Re-evaluate Negative Keywords and Match Types Given the decline in CTR (-18.5%) and overall clicks, it's crucial to review the search term report thoroughly. Identify irrelevant search queries that are consuming budget without converting and add them as negative keywords. Ensure that keyword match types are appropriately restrictive to prevent wasted spend on broad, non-converting traffic.
Facebook Ads
•Spend: THB 2,412.68 (+2.5%) •Impressions: 35,465 (-9.0%) •Clicks: 474 (-5.8%) •Leads: 10 (+150.0%) •CPA (Leads): ฿241.27 (-59.0%) •Messaging Conversations: 37 (-9.8%) •Cost per Messaging Conversation: ฿65.21 (+13.5%) ✅ Overall: Facebook Ads showed strong growth in leads with improved cost efficiency, but overall engagement and messaging conversations declined in September compared to August. Recommendation 1: Scale Successful Lead Generation Campaigns Leads increased significantly by 150.0%, and the Cost Per Lead (CPA) decreased by 59.0% in September. This indicates a successful lead generation strategy. Identify the specific campaigns and creatives responsible for this lead growth and consider scaling them up, reallocating budget to maximize this positive trend. Recommendation 2: Revitalize Messaging Conversation Campaigns Messaging conversations decreased by 9.8%, and the Cost per Messaging Conversation increased by 13.5% in September. The creative "อยากติดลิฟท์บ้าน" showed good performance with 31 conversations at a reasonable cost. Focus on improving the performance of messaging campaigns by reviewing ad creatives, calls to action, and targeting to drive more cost-effective conversations. Test new creative variations and audiences. Recommendation 3: Address Declining Reach and Engagement Impressions (-9.0%) and clicks (-5.8%) both declined in September, suggesting reduced reach and engagement. Analyze audience targeting, ad placement, and creative fatigue across all campaigns. Consider refreshing ad creatives or expanding audience targeting to improve overall reach and click-through rates.

Recommendation 1: Prioritize Content for Improving Keywords Keywords like "ลิฟท์" and "ลิฟต์โดยสาร" showed significant improvements in search ranking (position change +8 and +9 respectively) and traffic in September. Focus on creating more in-depth, high-quality content optimized for these terms and related variations to capitalize on their rising visibility and drive more organic traffic. Recommendation 2: Address Keywords with Declining Positions Several important keywords, such as "ลิฟต์ขนส่ง" (position change -4) and "ขนาดลิฟท์บ้าน" (position change -31), saw their rankings decline in September. Review the content associated with these keywords, update them for relevance and comprehensiveness, and implement link-building strategies to improve their authority and search engine visibility. Recommendation 3: Conduct Comprehensive Technical SEO Audit The significant drops in users, new users, and sessions, coupled with a total traffic change of -10.8%, suggest potential underlying technical SEO issues beyond individual keyword performance. Perform a thorough technical audit to check for site speed, mobile-friendliness, crawl errors, indexing issues, and schema markup to ensure the site is fully optimized for search engines. 🔎 Key Takeaways Google Ads requires immediate intervention due to rapidly increasing costs and declining conversions. Facebook Ads is effectively generating leads at a lower cost, but overall engagement and messaging performance needs optimization.
