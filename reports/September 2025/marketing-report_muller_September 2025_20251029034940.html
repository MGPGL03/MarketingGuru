Executive Insights

Google Ads
Recommendations: Google Ads experienced a significant drop in clicks (-34.0%) and conversions (-57.6%), alongside a substantial increase in CPA (฿160.36 from ฿63.00), despite an increase in spend (+7.9%). To mitigate this, immediately review keyword bidding strategies, especially for broad match types, and consider pausing underperforming keywords with high CPA. Prioritize allocating more budget and optimizing ad copy for high-converting search terms like "aritco lift ราคา" and "ลิ ฟ ท์ ขน สินค้า" to improve overall efficiency. ✅ Overall: Performance declined with fewer conversions at a much higher cost, indicating a need for urgent optimization.
Facebook Ads
Recommendations: Facebook Ads showed a remarkable 150.0% increase in leads with a 59.0% decrease in CPA (฿241.27 from ฿588.63), which is a positive development. However, impressions (-9.0%), clicks (-5.8%), and messaging conversations (-9.8%) decreased, while cost per messaging conversation increased (+13.5%). Capitalize on the successful lead generation by analyzing the performing audiences and creative elements, then scale these campaigns. Simultaneously, review and optimize creatives for messaging campaigns to re-engage users and reduce the cost per conversation. ✅ Overall: Strong performance in lead generation but a decline in overall engagement and increased cost for messaging conversations.
SEO
Recommendations: SEO saw a general decline in users (-27.3%), sessions (-27.1%), and organic sessions (-2.6%), leading to a -10.8% change in total traffic. Positively, the engagement rate improved by 8.2%. Focus on leveraging the keywords that showed improved rankings and traffic, such as "ลิฟท์" and "ลิฟต์โดยสาร," by developing more targeted and high-quality content around them. Conduct a thorough audit to identify the root causes of the overall traffic decline and implement technical SEO improvements and content refresh strategies to recover lost visibility. ✅ Overall: Key traffic metrics decreased, but an improved engagement rate and specific keyword gains present opportunities for strategic content and technical SEO efforts. 🔎 Key Takeaways Google Ads is performing inefficiently, with fewer conversions at higher costs. Facebook Ads is successfully delivering leads at a lower cost but is seeing reduced engagement in other areas. For the upcoming month, we should prioritize aggressive optimization of Google Ads to reduce CPA and boost conversions, scale the successful lead generation campaigns on Facebook, and strategically enhance SEO efforts around performing keywords while addressing overall traffic declines.
