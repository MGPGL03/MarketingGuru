Google Ads
Recommendations: Google Ads saw substantial growth from August to September with impressions up 313.6%, clicks up 286.4%, and spend increasing by 486.3%, resulting in 19 conversions in September compared to zero in August. While the Click-Through Rate (CTR) experienced a slight decrease of 6.6%, the 'Forex08' campaign demonstrated strong performance with 15 conversions at a cost per conversion of ฿954.69. To continue this positive trajectory, focus on further optimizing high-performing campaigns like 'Forex08' to improve conversion volume and efficiency, and analyze the CTR decline to ensure ad relevance and quality are maintained as spend increases.
Overall: The significant increase in conversions marks a strong performance shift for Google Ads from August to September, warranting continued investment and optimization for conversion-focused campaigns.
Facebook Ads
Recommendations: Facebook Ads experienced considerable growth in engagement from August to September, with impressions up 52.2%, clicks up 97.6%, and spend increasing by 86.0%, leading to a 29.8% increase in Click-Through Rate (CTR). However, leads decreased sharply by 46.7%, and the cost per lead surged from ฿906.29 in August to ฿3160.66 in September. Conversely, messaging conversations grew by 82.1% with a marginal 2.1% increase in cost per messaging conversation. It is crucial to immediately audit the lead generation campaigns to identify and resolve the issues causing the decrease in leads and the escalating cost per lead, while simultaneously exploring strategies to further enhance and monetize the successful messaging conversation channel.
Overall: While Facebook Ads excelled in driving engagement from August to September, the decline in leads and the associated cost increase are critical areas that require urgent attention, balanced with leveraging the promising growth in messaging conversations.
Key Takeaways
Both Google and Facebook ad spend increased considerably from August to September. Google Ads successfully scaled spend to drive a significant number of new conversions, establishing a solid foundation for return on investment. Facebook Ads, despite improved engagement metrics and increased spend, faced challenges in lead generation, indicating a need for a strategic re-evaluation of its lead acquisition approach, while its growth in messaging conversations presents an alternative avenue for customer interaction and potential conversion.
